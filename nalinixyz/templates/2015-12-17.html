Day six!

So you've officially graduated.

You are now officially smarter than me!

I often try to use my intelligence to defend my psyche

I sit behind this wall in my mind and pretend that there is nothing else to life, that being smart will get me all the way

You aren't like that.

Yes, of course you're smart, but you're so much more than just that.

You're observant: You notice immediately when people need help or extra love.

You're generous: When you notice that someone needs you, you put down everything in order to help them.

You're committed: You don't lay down when things get hard, you stay up and work 40 hours straight to meet the deadline or the requirement.

You're fun loving: You aren't afraid to sing along with the music or get up and dance while everyone else is sitting down.

You're fair: You want to always do your part and you expect others to do theirs as well.

You're sweet: You write cute poems and draw beautiful pictures.

You're precise: You fold everything perfectly and keep your bills organized at all times in your wallet.

You're understanding: You know when I've had a long day or a hard week and dont hold that against me.

You're courageous: You told you parents how you feel, even thought you knew they wouldn't approve.

You're brilliant: In so many more ways than I can describe, and I'm proud of you.
